Luxury Jewelry: Mangatta and Gallo


Your destinatoin for Exquisite Jewelry


Welcome to the world of elegance and enchantment at Mangata and Gallo, where dreams are transformed into timeless treasures. Our jewelry store specializes in curating exquisite pieces for life's most cherished moments, from engagements and weddings to anniversaries and beyond. With a blend of artistic mastery and meticulous craftsmanship, we bring your visions to life.

Meet Mariana,
the heart and soul behind Mangata and Gallo. A local luminary in Austin, Texas, Mariana embarked on a journey of creativity and craftsmanship after graduating from design school. Her specialization in diamond cutting and metal smithing has allowed her to create jewelry that radiates with brilliance and elegance. Mariana's designs are a fusion of classic sophistication and modern flair, reflecting her deep passion for celebrating life's most precious moments through her art.
